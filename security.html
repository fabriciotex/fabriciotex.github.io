Online Security

We worry about your online identity.

We worry about your safety online!

This page is to ensure that we treat your information with the most security possible, and making you understand the risks and how we take care of your information online.

There are a few properties that we access to ensure you have the best experience on our website making it free of bugs and making the right content to show up in the right place, however those informations can't cause any harm on your computer, just ensure your best experience in our website.

Property

Description

Gathered Information

navigator.onLine

This property will return your current network status to synchronize the latest information between our web server and your website.

navigator.platform

This property can access the Operating System you're using to access our website, and make us know how you better access it to ensure an excellent usability, being on desktop or mobile devices.

navigator.userAgent

This property give a better overview about the browser's name and version to ensure that our website will work properly with all our features enabled in your web browser.

navigator.geolocation

This property can access where in the world is the website is being accessed to find you our nearest location. It only works when authorized by you, the user.

screen.width

This property ensures that we can set the size of the page accordingly to the best experience in our website.

screen.height

This property, as the aforementioned, ensures that our website will load in the best size to ensure good user experience.
